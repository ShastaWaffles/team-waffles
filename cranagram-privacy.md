# Cranagram The Discord Activity - Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: December 2025**

Cranagram The Discord Activity ("we," "our," "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Discord Activity app.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Discord User Data
When you use Cranagram, we collect the following information provided by Discord's API:
- **User ID**: Your unique Discord user identifier
- **Username**: Your Discord username
- **Display Name**: Your Discord global display name (if set)
- **Avatar Hash**: Your Discord avatar identifier (for displaying your profile picture)
- **Guild ID**: The Discord server/guild ID where you use the app

### Game Activity Data
We collect and store the following game-related information:
- **Puzzle ID**: Identifier for the puzzle you're solving
- **Score**: Your score for each puzzle
- **Attempts**: Number of attempts made to solve the puzzle
- **Solve Status**: Whether you successfully solved the puzzle
- **Completion Time**: Time taken to solve the puzzle (in milliseconds)
- **Solution Word**: The word you used to solve the puzzle (if solved)
- **Timestamp**: When you submitted your score

### Local Storage Data
The app uses browser localStorage to store:
- **Guess History**: Your previous guesses for each puzzle (stored locally on your device, not on our servers)

### What We Do NOT Collect
We do **not** collect:
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Payment information
- Location data
- Messages or chat content
- Any data beyond what is necessary for the app's core functionality

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide core functionality**: Display leaderboards, track scores, and manage game state
- **Improve performance**: Identify and fix technical issues
- **Maintain fair play**: Prevent abuse and ensure a fair gaming experience
- **Display user information**: Show your username, avatar, and scores on leaderboards

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

- **Storage Location**: Data is stored in a SQLite database on our servers
- **Retention Period**: Game scores and related data are retained indefinitely to maintain leaderboard history and game statistics
- **Data Updates**: Your username, display name, and avatar hash are updated when you submit new scores to keep information current
- **Local Storage**: Guess history stored in your browser's localStorage remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers

## 4. Data Sharing

- **We do not sell your data** to third parties
- **We do not share your data** with third parties except:
  - As required by law or legal process
  - To protect our rights or prevent abuse
- **Discord Integration**: We use Discord's API to authenticate users and retrieve basic profile information, subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
- **Third-Party Services**: The app integrates with cranagram.com for puzzle content, but we do not share your personal data with them

## 5. Data Security

- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data
- However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure
- We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information

## 6. User Rights and Control

You have the right to:
- **Stop using the app** at any time by removing it from your server
- **Request information** about what data we have stored about you
- **Request deletion** of your data (contact us via Discord)

**Note**: Deleting your data will remove your scores from leaderboards. This action may be irreversible.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Cranagram is intended for users 13 years and older, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
- We will indicate the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document
- Continued use of Cranagram after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
- Significant changes will be communicated when possible

## 9. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, you can contact us at: **@wafflesaresomething** on Discord.

*This Privacy Policy is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy and Developer Policy. By using Cranagram, you also agree to Discord's data handling practices as outlined in their policies.*
